At Alma College, we’ll help you chart a path for your future. If you don’t graduate on time following the plan for your major, we’ll pay your tuition for an extra term. You read that right — it’s our commitment to you.

Discover your world: the Alma Venture

You won’t just get a degree, you’ll get experience. You design an amazing learning opportunity — such as study abroad, a research fellowship or an internship — and we will give you up to $2,500 to support your experience. See what other students have done.

Our admissions and financial aid staff are available to sit down with you and answer your questions. We want to help you make the best decision (it’s no secret; we hope you choose Alma!).

  • In the past year, Alma College awarded over $24.1 million in scholarships to its students.
  • Every student receives some level of award.
  • Get individualized assistance from your admissions rep.
  • With merit scholarships, special awards and need-based grants, we are working to make the quality education that Alma provides accessible to you.
